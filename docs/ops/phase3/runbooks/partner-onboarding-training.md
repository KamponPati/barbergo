# Phase 3 Partner Onboarding and Training

## Goal
Enable pilot partners with verified documents, operational SOP, and dry-run completion before going live.

## Entry Criteria
- Partner KYC status is `approved`
- Branch setup complete (open hours, capacity, service list, staff shifts)
- Wallet payout destination validated

## Training Flow
1. Product walkthrough: incoming queue, confirm/reject/reschedule, start/complete booking.
2. Exception handling drill: no-show, late staff, customer cancellation.
3. Finance drill: wallet summary, withdrawal request, refund impact.
4. Compliance drill: policy acknowledgement and audit log awareness.

## Readiness Checklist
- [x] Staff can confirm booking within SLA target
- [x] Staff can complete service and close booking
- [x] Staff can process reschedule and explain policy
- [x] Staff can escalate disputes with evidence
- [x] Staff completed shadow shift with ops lead

## Owners
- Ops lead: pilot operations manager
- Product support: partner success
- Engineering support: on-call backend engineer
