# Phase 3 Support Playbook (Incident/Dispute/No-show)

## Incident Severity
- SEV-1: payment fail or booking state corruption affecting many users
- SEV-2: zone outage or notification delay over 15 minutes
- SEV-3: isolated partner/customer issue with workaround

## Response SLA
- Acknowledge SEV-1 within 5 minutes
- Acknowledge SEV-2 within 10 minutes
- Acknowledge SEV-3 within 30 minutes

## Incident Workflow
1. Receive alert from admin feed or support channel.
2. Open incident ticket with request IDs and booking IDs.
3. Mitigate impact (freeze affected zone or partner if needed).
4. Communicate status every 15 minutes (SEV-1/2).
5. Close with root cause and corrective action.

## Dispute Workflow
1. Validate evidence timeline and policy matrix reference.
2. Assign owner from trust/safety queue.
3. Apply resolution action: refund, partial credit, or reject claim.
4. Update dispute record and notify customer/partner.

## No-show Workflow
1. Verify partner/customer timeline in audit feed.
2. Classify no-show owner by policy rules.
3. Apply cancellation/no-show fee and release slot.
4. Log repeated abuse for trust threshold review.
